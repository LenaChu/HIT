Draw The Outline of A Dog

  1. In this task, you will be asked to draw the outline of a dog on an image.
  2. Click your mouse to create small squares on the boundary of a bus. The squares will be connected to make outline.
  3. Press any key to undo.
  4. Make the outline very tight to the boundary.
  5. Remember to make a close polygon.
  6. Type your comment(optional).
  7. Press the Submit button to submit the result.
